Kimchi

With a little prep, this easy Kimchi recipe only takes 30 minutes before mother nature takes over and youre left with healthy, gut healing, probiotic packed, tasty kimchi.

Ingredients

  • 1 large nappa cabbage
  • ginger
  • garlic
  • scallions
  • daikon
  • shrimp paste, fish sauce, dried shrimp or miso paste
  • gochugaru or gochujang
  • glutenous rice flour (optional to thicken the mixture)

Instructions

  1. Save 1-2 outer leaves and place in the fridge for later. Cut the rest of the cabbage into 1-inch cubes.
  2. Place cabbage into a large bowl and toss with 1/4 cup sea salt and mix well before filling the bowl with filtered water and stirring. Allow cabbage to soak for 6-8 hours and save the brine.
  3. In a food processor, make a paste with the garlic, ginger, half the scallions, gochugaru, fish sauce, and sugar.
  4. Peel and julienne the daikon and cut the remaining scallions.
  5. Combine the Kimchi by massaging well with gloved hands until well mixed.
  6. Pack the mixture tight into glass jars or a large crock, fill to the top with brine, and cover with spare cabbage leaves
  7. Allow the kimchi to ferment somewhere dark and cool for 3-5 days and enjoy!
