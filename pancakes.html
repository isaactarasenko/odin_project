Pancakes

Pancakes are a simple and delicious household staple, perfect for weekend breakfasts.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 3/4 cup milk
  • 1 tsp vanilla essence
  • 2 cups self-raising flour
  • 1/3 cup caster sugar
  • butter, for frying, plus extra to serve
  • maple syrup to serve

Instructions

  1. Whisk eggs, milk and vanilla together in a jug. Sift flour into a large bowl and stir in the sugar. Make a well in the centre of the bowl and whisk in mixture until just combined
  2. Heat a large pan over medium heat and grease with butter. Add roughly 1/4 cup mixture to pan per pancake, and cook for 2 minutes each side or until a good number of bubbles cover the surface
  3. Transger to a plate, cover to keep warm and repeat to finish the rest of the mixture.
  4. Serve with maple syrup and butter and enjoy!
